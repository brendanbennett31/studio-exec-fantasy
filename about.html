ABOUT

Studio Exec Fantasy is a free, non-commercial fantasy league for film people. No ads, no subscriptions, no monetization. Just a fun way to compete at the box office.

What Is This?

Studio Exec Fantasy is a season-long competition where each exec drafts a slate of films competing for domestic box office earnings and Oscar nominations. Films releasing between Oscar Sunday and Oscar Sunday the following year are eligible. At the end of the season, whoever has the highest total — box office plus Oscar bonuses plus unspent budget — wins the league.

This is a free project built for fun by a group of film people. It is not affiliated with any studio, awards organization, or industry body. All box office data is sourced from public box office records. Oscar odds are sourced from Gold Derby.

Studio Exec Fantasy is non-commercial. We do not sell your data, run ads, or charge for any features. Everything here is free, always.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I join a league?
Ask your league admin to share the invite link. Open the link, create an account or sign in, and you'll be added to the league automatically.
How is box office tracked?
Domestic box office figures are pulled from public records and updated daily. Numbers reflect cumulative domestic gross as reported.
How do Oscar bonuses work?
Each Oscar nomination earns $25M. Each win earns an additional $25M. A Best Picture win earns an additional $50M on top of the nomination and win bonuses. Bonus amounts can vary by league — check your league's Rules tab for exact figures.
What happens if a film gets voided?
A film is voided if it moves outside the Oscar eligibility window, releases on streaming only, or if a festival future pick was already claimed by another exec. Voided picks lose the bid amount with no refund.
What are Festival Futures?
Festival Futures let you draft the winner of major film festivals (Cannes Palme d'Or, TIFF People's Choice, etc.) before the winner is announced. High risk, high reward.
Can I be in multiple leagues?
Yes. Your dashboard shows all your leagues. If you're in multiple leagues, you'll land on the dashboard when you log in so you can pick which one to open.
How do I delete my account?
Use the feedback form below and select "Account deletion request." We'll remove your data within 48 hours.
Is my data private?
Your email and account details are stored securely via Supabase and are never sold or shared. League data (picks, standings) is visible to other members of your leagues.
