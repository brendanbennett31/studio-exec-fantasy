The Game

HOW IT WORKS

Studio Exec Fantasy is a season-long competition where each player manages a slate of films like a real studio exec — with a budget, real stakes, and one year to prove their taste makes money.

01

Draft Your Slate

Each exec starts with a $500M budget and bids on films releasing in the Oscar-to-Oscar window. Any film releasing in that window is fair game — tent poles, prestige, wild cards.

AUCTION DRAFT
02

Earn at the Box Office

Every dollar your films earn at the domestic box office goes directly onto your scoreboard. A $10M bid on a $200M hit? That's the whole game right there.

LIVE TRACKING
03

Stack Awards

Oscar nominations and wins convert to bonus earnings at the end of the season. A Best Picture win is worth $50M. Every other win or nomination adds $25M each.

OSCAR BONUSES
04

Unspent Budget Pays Out

Every dollar you don't spend during the draft carries over into your total earnings. Savvy budgeting is a real competitive advantage — don't overbid.

BUDGET STRATEGY
05

Festival Futures

Bid on the winner of Cannes, TIFF's People's Choice, or other major festivals before the winner is announced. High risk, high reward — if you're right.

FESTIVAL PICKS
06

Winner Takes the Pot

Highest total earnings at the end of the Oscar season wins. Your total is box office + Oscar bonuses + unspent budget. The exec with the best eye wins it all.

WINNER TAKES ALL
